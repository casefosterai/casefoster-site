OnTime adds historical on-time performance and cancellation data to every flight option on Google Flights. The data comes from the US Department of Transportation's Bureau of Transportation Statistics, the same source the DOT uses for its monthly Air Travel Consumer Report. Instead of picking a flight based on price and schedule alone, you can see how often that exact route and airline actually arrives on time.

Works on Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Arc, and other Chromium-based browsers via the Chrome Web Store.
